description: "Three live specialists + a lead. Each watches one signal it can verify."
---

A scout watches **one signal** and grades only what it can verify. If the signal is missing, the scout returns `null` (Unknown) — not zero. The lead synthesizes the four scouts into the final ranked pick.

| Scout | Watches | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **On-chain** | Mint authority, freeze authority, pair age | ✅ live |
| **Liquidity** | Pool size (USD), liquidity-to-FDV ratio | ✅ live |
| **Momentum** | Buy/sell pressure + 24h change, **weighted by volume** | ✅ live |
| **Smart-money** | Notable / clustered wallet entries | 🔜 v1.5 |
| **Scout lead** | All scout outputs → ranked shortlist | ✅ live |

---

## On-chain scout

**Watches** — `mint_authority` and `freeze_authority` from a Solana RPC `getAccountInfo` on the mint account, plus `pair_age` (hours since pair creation).

**Scoring rules:**

| Authority state | Score | Read |
|---|---|---|
| Both renounced | **85** | "Mint & freeze authority renounced" |
| One renounced, one live | **55** | "One authority still live — partial risk" |
| Both live | **20** | "Mint/freeze authority live — rug risk" |
| Both unverifiable | **Unknown** | excluded from composite, added to `unknowns` |

**Why it matters** — a token with live mint or freeze authority can be inflated (mint) or frozen (freeze) by the dev at any time. Renouncing both is the on-chain commitment that the float is fixed and tradeable.

## Liquidity scout

**Watches** — `liquidity_usd` from DexScreener, falling back to **Jupiter's bonding-curve liquidity** when DexScreener has nothing (essential for fresh launchpad tokens).

**Scoring** — `_scale(liquidity_usd, $50000)`: linear up to $50k → score 100, with floor 0.

**Caveats:**

- If liquidity is `None` after both sources, the scout returns `null` and **caps the overall composite at 70** — a token can't read as "clean" with unverifiable liquidity.
- Liquidity below $10k flips the standout risk to "Thin liquidity — exit-rug risk."

**Why it matters** — liquidity is the only thing that lets you actually sell. A token with $500 LP cannot absorb a $5k exit without massive slippage.

## Momentum scout

**Watches** — 1h buy/sell tx counts (DexScreener with Jupiter fallback) + 24h price change (Jupiter) + 24h volume (DexScreener).

**Scoring formula:**

```
pressure   = 50 + (buy_sell_ratio - 1) × 20      # 1.0x → 50, 3.5x → 100, clamped
pressure  += clamp(-25, +25, price_change_24h / 4)
confidence = min(1.0, volume_24h / $25k)         # ~$25k 24h = full weight
momentum   = pressure × (0.45 + 0.55 × confidence)
```

The **volume-weighted confidence** is the key honesty trick: a 9:1 buy/sell ratio on $500/24h is noise, not signal. The formula recognizes that.

**Worked example — $MRDN at low volume:**

- buy/sell ratio 9.0 → raw pressure 100
- 24h change −50% → pressure −13 → 87 then clamped
- volume $651 → confidence 0.026
- momentum = 87 × (0.45 + 0.55 × 0.026) ≈ **40 → 8 after rounding**

Compared with what a naive "ratio × scaling" would say (100/100), this is honest.

## The scout lead

The lead reads the four scout outputs, weights them against the [rubric](scoring-rubric), and emits the final pick. It's **bound by the [honesty rules](honesty-model)**:

1. Unknown stays Unknown.
2. The standout risk on a positive pick must be named.
3. "Worth investigating," never "buy."
4. Absence of evidence is a mild negative, not neutral.

In the live mode, the lead is a Swarms cloud agent with the system prompt that ships with the [marketplace listing](https://swarms.world). In the deterministic mode (used by `/evaluate`), the lead is the `_score_candidate()` function in [`backend/meridian/scouts/swarm.py`](https://github.com/Enoch208/Meridian/blob/main/backend/meridian/scouts/swarm.py).
