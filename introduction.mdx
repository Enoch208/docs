## What it is

Meridian is a swarm of specialized scout agents that continuously scans new Solana token launches, scores each against a transparent rubric, and surfaces the few worth investigating today — with a public, running track record of every call.

Most agents tell you whether a coin you *already found* is safe. **Meridian tells you which coins are even worth looking at in the first place.**

## What makes it different

| | One-shot due-diligence | Meridian |
|---|---|---|
| **Job** | Evaluate a token you bring | Discover tokens you didn't know about |
| **Stance** | Defensive — "is it safe?" | Offensive — "where's the edge?" |
| **Cadence** | One-shot, on demand | Daily, recurring |
| **Memory** | None | Public, running track record |

The **public track record** is the moat — it's the thing a paste-a-token verdict structurally can't have.
