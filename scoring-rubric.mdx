## Composite score

Every token gets a single **composite score (0–100)** derived from the live scouts:

| Score | Tier | Read |
|---|---|---|
| **75 – 100** | Strong | Clean signals across the board |
| **55 – 74** | Mixed | Mixed signals with upside — investigate with care |
| **0 – 54** | Weak | Weak or unverified signals — investigate cautiously |

The composite is the average of the **available** sub-scores. Unknowns are excluded so missing data doesn't pretend to be a 0.

## Sub-scores

| Scout | How it's scored |
|---|---|
| **On-chain** | 85 if both mint + freeze renounced · 55 if one · 20 if both live · **Unknown** if neither verifiable |
| **Liquidity** | `liquidity_usd` scaled against $50k · **Unknown** if no source has it |
| **Momentum** | Buy/sell pressure + 24h price change, **weighted by 24h volume** (a 9:1 ratio on $500/day reads thin, not perfect) |
| **Smart-money** | Always **Unknown** in v1; dedicated scout ships in v1.5 |

<Tip>
  Unknown liquidity **caps the overall composite at 70** — a token can't read as "clean" when its biggest risk signal is unverifiable.
</Tip>

## Honesty rules

These are baked into every agent's system prompt and the deterministic scorer alike:

1. **Unknown is Unknown.** If a signal can't be verified, it's marked Unknown and down-weighted — never invented.
2. **"Worth investigating," never "buy."** Every pick is framed as research, not advice.
3. **Always name the standout risk.** Omitting it is a scoring error.
4. **Absence of evidence is a mild negative**, not a neutral.

The rules are the product's reason to exist — without them, the track record is worth nothing.

## What gets surfaced on each pick

Every pick — daily shortlist or on-demand `/evaluate` — carries:

- The **composite score**, plus each sub-score (with `null` for Unknown).
- **Top reasons** the scouts surfaced (2 strongest).
- The **standout risk** (always present on a positive pick).
- A **plain-English one-line read**.
- The list of **unknowns** (so the gaps are explicit).
- Full **market metrics** (price, 24h change, liquidity, FDV/MCAP, volume, age, holders, top-holders %, dev-wallet %, organic score, mint/freeze auth, launchpad, socials).
